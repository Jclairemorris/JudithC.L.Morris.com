Victoria's Secret – Semi-Annual Sale

Semi-Annual Sale journey spanning mobile email, desktop hub, and tablet category grid. Creative is tuned for replenishment shoppers, high-impact photography, and clear promotional framing to lift conversion and AOV.

Mobile hero email kicking off the Semi-Annual Sale with bold photography and $10-off framing for full-priced bras.

Email delivered 45% of all online conversions and helped lift overall campaign conversion by +4.0%.

Desktop sale hub carrying through core typography, tiered offers, and direct paths into bras, panties, and sleep.

Hub contributed to $18.5M in SAS revenue and supported an +8% lift in AOV through bundling and threshold offers.

Tablet-optimized category grid connecting promo tiles to PDPs for couch, bed, or on-the-go browsing.

Grid kept tablet shoppers converting in line with mobile, while supporting over 180M cross-channel impressions.
